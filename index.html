About Me

Hello! Once again, I am a sophomore computer science major at Penn State. I came from a smallish high school with about 600 people total, so when I first arrived at college, I was in awe of the sheer size and atmosphere. The college life felt so daunting since I had to be more independent and make myself seen on a vast campus.


But once I got to know more people and join more extracurriculars, I was able to feel right at home. I joined the Penn State Filipino Associate (PSFA) my freshman year, which was one of the best decisions I could have made. Not only was I able to keep in touch with my culture more, but I was also able to make many lasting friendships with other people who were also like me. I was also able to expand on my programming knowledge as well by becoming a member of the Association for Computing Machinery. I even landed an executive position where I taught members of the club web development! And finally, I am also in an organization that supports THON, the 46 hour marathon and fundraiser for pediatric cancer. I hope to have many more positive memories and experiences at Penn State!
